Mission

At Orangopus, we are dedicated to exploring the vast expanse of creativity like the infinite cosmos, providing an open-source, non-profit platform where every mind can launch its ideas into the stars. Our mission is to cultivate a universe of inclusivity and innovation, where creators from all backgrounds can navigate their journey of development, guided by mentorship and powered by the freedom of open-source technology.
